Featured Publications

Matthew Stanley, Lingfeng Tao, Xiaoli Zhang
December, 2021 Proc. in IEEE ROBIO
Robust Motion Mapping Between Human and Humanoids Using CycleAutoencoder

Using a new transferability metric with our new, cyclically designed autoencoder, we developed a framework for producing more robust motion mapping for intuitive robot teleoperation.

PDF Cite
Matthew Stanley, Michael Bowman, Xiaoli Zhang, Jeffery King
October, 2021 Proc. in ICAPP
Data-Driven Uncertainty-Aware Nuclear Power Plant Sensor Modeling

We created a uncertainty-aware data-driven model to filter noise and reduce sensor uncertainty inside of a trigger reactor.

Cite
Matthew Stanley, Julien Hajjar, Timothy Fitzgerald
April, 2018 In SIRC
Effects of Uniaxial Tensile Strain on Moth Wings Through Digital Image Correlation

We studied the effects of uniaxial tensile testing on Hawkmoth wings. Using a high-speed camera, digital image correlation using the patterns on the wings allowed us to measure the Young’s Modulus and Poisson’s ration across sections of the wings.
